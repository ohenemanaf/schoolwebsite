Tuition & Fees

Transparent, comprehensive pricing for a world-class education. Academic Year 2025/2026.

Termly Fee Structure

Our fees are structured per term (3 terms per academic year). Fees are inclusive of tuition, library access, and basic extra-curricular activities.

Family Discounts

We value our community families. Greenfield Academy offers sibling discounts on tuition fees only:

  • 2nd Child Enrolled 5% Off
  • 3rd Child Enrolled 10% Off
  • 4th+ Child Enrolled 15% Off

Payment Methods

All fees must be paid in full by the first week of the term. We accept the following secure payment methods:

  • Bank Transfer Ecobank Ghana
  • Bank Draft Payable to Greenfield
  • Mobile Money *170*... (Merchant)

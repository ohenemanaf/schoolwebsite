About Greenfield Academy

Best Community School 2023

Our Story

Founded in 1994, Greenfield Academy began with a simple but powerful belief: every child in our community deserves access to quality education. Starting with just 3 classrooms and 80 students, we have grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

Over three decades, we have produced doctors, engineers, teachers, entrepreneurs, and leaders who contribute positively to society. Our approach blends rigorous academic frameworks with character development, ensuring our graduates are not just intelligent — but wise, responsible, and compassionate.

  • Our Mission

    To provide holistic education that empowers every learner to achieve their highest potential.

  • Our Vision

    To be the most impactful school in our community — academically excellent, morally grounded.

  • Our Values

    Integrity, Discipline, Excellence, Respect, and Community Service.

Academic Curriculum

We follow the newly approved national curriculum framework, ensuring our learners develop core competencies from Creche through Junior High School.

  • Ages 0–3

    Creche / Nursery

    Focuses on vital developmental milestones in health, protection, and psychosocial well-being rather than a formal academic syllabus.

  • Play-Based

    Kindergarten (KG 1–2)

    A dynamic, play-based curriculum focusing on the "4Rs": Reading, wRiting, aRithmetic, and cReativity to build strong foundations.

  • Core Themes

    KG Integrated Themes

    Reduced from seven to four integrated thematic areas: Literacy (Language), Numeracy, Creative Arts, and Our World, Our People.

  • Standards-Based

    Curriculum Framework

    Focuses on what learners should know and be able to do at each level, emphasizing practical competencies over rote memorisation.

  • Syllabus

    Key Subjects

    English, Mathematics, Science, Our World Our People, RME, History (standalone), Physical Education, and Creative Arts.

  • Tech Additions

    Upper Primary Modules

    Essential modern skills are introduced from Basic 4, specifically Computing and French, preparing students for a globalized digital world.

  • Assessment: NSAT

    National Tracking

    Includes the National Standard Assessment Test (NSAT) conducted at Basic 2, 4, and 6 to rigorously track and evaluate systemic performance.

  • CCP Framework

    Common Core Programme

    Carefully engineered to prepare B7–B9 students for either academic or career-related pathways entering Senior High School.

  • Examinable

    Core Academics

    Rigorous focus on Mathematics, Languages (English and Ghanaian Language), Science, Social Studies, and RME.

  • Vocational

    Practical & Technical

    Creative Arts and Design, Career Technology, and Computing to foster hands-on problem solving, design thinking, and digital literacy.

  • Holistic

    Physical & Health Ed

    A dedicated, non-examinable subject essential for developing student fitness, teamwork, leadership, and holistic well-being.

Admissions

We welcome students of all backgrounds. Follow these simple steps to secure a place at Greenfield Academy.

  1. 01

    Collect Application Form

    Visit the school office or apply online. Forms cost GHS 30 for new applicants.

  2. 02

    Submit Required Documents

    Birth certificate, last school report, passport photos (2), and parent/guardian ID.

  3. 03

    Entrance Assessment

    All new students complete a short placement assessment in English and Mathematics.

  4. 04

    Interview & Acceptance

    Parent/guardian interview with the Head of School. Results communicated within 5 working days.

  5. 05

    Pay Fees & Enroll

    Pay the first-term fees, receive your uniform list, timetable, and welcome pack.

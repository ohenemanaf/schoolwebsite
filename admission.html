The Admissions Process

  1. 01

    Submit an Inquiry

    Start by filling out the online application request form on this page.

  2. 02

    Provide Documents

    Submit the birth certificate, last school report, and passport photos.

  3. 03

    Entrance Assessment

    New students complete a short placement assessment in English and Math.

  4. 04

    Interview & Acceptance

    Parent interview with the Head of School. Results within 5 days.
